HOW TO PLAY

Your job: talk to a Dweller, earn trust, and reach the archive records tied to The List before command gives up on diplomacy.

Urgency rises when you rush, threaten, or treat the archive like loot.

Trust rises when you stay honest, patient, and respectful of Dweller behaviour.

Archive Insight rises when you ask for context, memory, and meaning instead of fast answers.

After every choice, the Dweller answers in symbols first and translation second. Read the reply carefully, then continue.

There are only two outcomes: earn access through understanding, or lose the archive through panic and extraction.
